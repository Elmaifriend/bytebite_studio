FAQ

What kind of services does your software agency offer?

We offer web and mobile app development, landing page design, online stores, digital transformation and customized technology consultancies.

How long does it take to develop an app?

The time depends on the complexity of the project. Typically, basic web and mobile applications can take anywhere from 4 to 12 weeks, while larger projects may require more time.

How is the price of a project established?

Cost is based on complexity, specific requirements, and the time needed to complete the project. We offer customized quotes after a free initial consultation.

Can I make modifications to the application after it has been released?

Of course! We offer maintenance and support services to implement updates, enhancements or new features after release.

Do you offer e-commerce solutions?

Yes, we develop online stores with payment integration, product and inventory management, as well as optimized designs to improve sales.

Do you perform digital transformation projects?

Yes, we assist companies to modernize their processes, adopting technologies that will improve efficiency, lower costs and increase productivity.

Can you work on a project that has already begun?

Yes, we can continue with ongoing projects, evaluating the current status and proposing improvements or developing new functionalities.

How involved will I be in the development process?

We keep constant and transparent communication throughout the entire process. You'll be involved at every key stage, from planning to final release.

Do you offer consultancy services?

Yes, we offer technology consultancy services to help you take well-informed decisions on the development, improvement or adoption of new technologies.

How do I start a project with you?

You can contact us for an initial consultation, where we'll discuss your requirements and goals. From there, we'll provide you with a detailed proposal.
